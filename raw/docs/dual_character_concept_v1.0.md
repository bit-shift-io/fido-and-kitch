# 2D Platformer Character Art Prompt (Bipedal / Upright)

## Updated Prompt Text

2D character concept design featuring two animal best friends: a cute cartoon dog and a cute cartoon cat, standing upright on two legs (bipedal human-like posture) side-by-side in a Bluey-inspired vector art style with oversized heads.

* **Proportions & Anatomy:** Ultra-cute chibi/Bluey proportions with giant oversized heads (1:1.5 head-to-body ratio), small chubby torso, short sausage-like limbs, and soft rounded paws. **Bipedal stance standing upright on two hind legs like a human**, with arms at their sides, designed for a 2D platformer character sheet.

* **Character 1 (The Dog):**
  * **Description:** Enthusiastic, playful puppy standing upright on two legs, with large floppy ears, a short stubby tail, big expressive round eyes, and an open-mouthed happy smile.
  * **Colors:** Warm golden-yellow fur, soft cream chest patch, dark chocolate-brown ears, wearing a bright sky-blue collar.

* **Character 2 (The Cat):**
  * **Description:** Clever, curious cat standing upright on two legs, with large perky triangular ears, a long graceful curving tail, wide almond-shaped eyes, and a cute "3"-shaped cat mouth.
  * **Colors:** Vibrant orange tabby coat with white paws and chest, soft pink inner ears, striking lime-green eyes, wearing a bright turquoise bandana.

* **Art Style & Rendering:** Crisp 2D vector graphic, bold dark rounded outlines, flat saturated color fills, subtle soft highlights, zero blur, ultra-clean edges optimized for high readability in a 2D platformer.

* **Composition:** Full-body 2D platformer character concept sheet, front-three-quarters view, **both characters standing fully upright on two feet** and interacting playfully, isolated on a clean solid off-white background.

* **Negative Prompt:** Quadrupedal, walking on four legs, all-fours pose, photorealistic 3D render, dark gritty lighting, realistic fur textures, thin sketchy lines, realistic human body proportions, small heads, muted washed-out pastel colors.
